My journey as a bootcamp student

I started my journey on Scrimba with a clear goal: to become a confident, job-ready web developer. Along the way, I've built a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and tackled hands-on projects. It's been challenging at times—especially learning solo—but every completed module and mini-project has boosted both my skills and confidence.
