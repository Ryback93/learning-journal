The journey so far.

I was on the hunt for a career that combined creativity with practicality—something that aligned with my passions and gave me room to grow. Having always enjoyed working with computers, web development seemed like the perfect match. It’s hands-on, fast-paced, and offers endless opportunities to create something from scratch.

After bouncing around a few online resources, I finally stumbled upon Scrimba, and it completely changed the game for me. Their interactive approach kept me motivated and made the material stick. The moment I completed my first fully functional project, I knew I was exactly where I was meant to be.
